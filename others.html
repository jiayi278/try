Introduction

Welcome to the rich tapestry of indigenous cultures nestled within the lush landscapes of Sarawak and Sabah, where ancient traditions and vibrant communities thrive amidst the beauty of nature. Among these communities such as the Iban, Kadazan, and Bidayuh peoples, each with their own unique customs, languages, and artistic expressions. Through their art, music, and rituals, the indigenous continue to celebrate their cultural identity and inspire others to appreciate the beauty and diversity of Malaysia's indigenous heritage.
