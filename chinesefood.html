Top 3 Must Try Chinese Food in Malaysia

1. Penang Char-Kuey-Teow

Penang Char Kuey Teow is a signature Malaysian dish originating from Penang, renowned for its savory stir-fried flat rice noodles cooked over high heat with prawns, cockles, Chinese lap cheong sausage, eggs, bean sprouts, and chives, seasoned with soy sauce and chili paste, creating a tantalizing blend of flavors and textures emblematic of the region's culinary prowess.
