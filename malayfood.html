Top 3 Must Try Malay Dishes in Malaysia

1. Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak is a beloved Malaysian dish, featuring fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaves, served with a variety of accompaniments such as crispy fried anchovies, roasted peanuts, cucumber slices, hard-boiled or fried egg, and spicy sambal sauce. This iconic dish represents a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, epitomizing the richness and diversity of Malaysian cuisine.
